=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:VAPORS:REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE.KEEP WARM,QUIET.NOTIFY PHYSICIAN.
    SPLASH IN EYES:FLUSH IMMED W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF H*2O FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES.TAKE TO PHYSICIAN FOR MEDICAL TREATMENT. SPLASH ON
    SKIN:WASH AF FECTED AREAS W/LRG AMTS H*2O.REMOVE CONTAM CLOTHES

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================
