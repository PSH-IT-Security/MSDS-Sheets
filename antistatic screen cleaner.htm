    EYELIDS OPEN. GET MD IF IRRIT PERSISTS. SKIN:FLUSH W/WATER.

    GIVE O*2 IF B RTHG IS DFCLT. INGEST:GET PROMPT MED HELP. GIVE WATER

    LG & VICTIM IS CONSCIOUS & ALERT, INDUCE VOMIT.
