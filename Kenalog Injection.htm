=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE:FLUSH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR 15-20 MIN. SKIN:WASH
    W/SOAP & WATER FOR 15-20 MIN. INGESTION:GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR; IF DIFFICULTY IN BREATHING
    ACMINISTER OXYGEN.	IF A PERSON IS NOT BREATHING ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION CAN BE ATTEMPTED. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.
